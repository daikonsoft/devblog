What is DaikonScramble?
DaikonScramble combines the endless wave, single stage gameplay of the classic 1983 Mario Bros arcade game, the movement and fighting mechanics of Super Smash Bros, bunnies, and an ever-expanding elevator shaft. It's a passion project born from a desire to create something meaningful and creative.
