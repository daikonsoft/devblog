DaikonScramble

What is DaikonScramble?

DaikonScramble is a bunny inspired platform beat-em-up incorporating tower defence elements, and a elevator based progression system where you descend down a seemingly bottomless burrow.

It was created from the desire to combine the endless wave, single stage elements of the classic 1983 Mario Bros arcade game, The movement and fighting mechanics of Super Smash Bros, Bunnies, and an ever expanding elevator shaft.

At least that is the case on paper, but the real reason for it's creation is that my partner (electronelle) and I (monomori) were becoming increasingly frustrated with our jobs, the state of the world, and things holding us back from creating something meaningful and creative. So in a sense the original idea we had was "let's make something purely because we want to, and not because anything else is saying we should."
